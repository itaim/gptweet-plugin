ChatGPT Twitter Plugin

SocialAssistant - a ChatGPT Twitter Plugin that enables ChatGPT to interact with your Twitter account, allowing you to manage your tweets, reply to others, search tweets, and more.

Usage Examples

  • Compose and post a new tweet with ChatGPT's assistance
  • Reply to a tweet in a specific thread
  • Search for tweets containing a specific keyword
  • Get a list of your followers or people you follow
  • View a user's profile information

Benefits

  • Easy integration with ChatGPT
  • Manage your Twitter account with the help of ChatGPT's AI capabilities
  • Improve your social media presence with more engaging content

Installation and Requirements

To use the ChatGPT Twitter Plugin, you need the following:

  • A ChatGPT Plus account
  • A Twitter account

To get started, install the SocialMediaAssistant Plugin from the ChatGPT plugin store, login with your Twitter account and post a Tweet from the chat interface.
